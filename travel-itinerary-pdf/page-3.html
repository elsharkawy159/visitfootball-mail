You will receive confirmation by email no later than 5 days before departure about how you will receive the match tickets. This can be done in one of the following ways.
Membercard:
Your admission ticket is loaded onto a plastic card that grants access to the stadium. Member cards are not in your name but can be used by you. You will receive instructions for use with these member cards with a brief explanation. The passes must be returned to us.
(Digital) Mobile tickets:
You will receive match tickets in the form of a link. By clicking on this link you can download your match tickets into your Wallet on a phone. Precise instructions on how this works will be sent with the tickets
(Digital) E-tickets:
These are digital files in PDF format that you can print at home and take with you to the stadium.
Hospitality tickets:
In many cases, these exclusive tickets also offer access to a lounge or bar and in some cases to a pre-match dinner. You will receive further explanation with these tickets.
