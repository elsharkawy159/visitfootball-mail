CHECK-IN & AIRLINES OVERVIEW
Check-In
You have to check in by yourself through the website of the airline.
If you only check in at the airport, an airline may charge additional fees.
You can check in with your booking number, your name and in some cases with the e-mail address info@voetbaltrips.com.
Below you can find an overview of the websites from the airlines. Please check all details carefully during check-in.
As a traveler you are responsible for checking your boarding pass. If you are not admitted to the aircraft due to an error in the flight details, we take no responsibility if you have not informed us in advance of the presence of this error.
